# Identity & Positioning — Sapore è Sapere

## The name is the brand

**Sapore è Sapere** literally means *"Flavor is Knowledge."* This is not a tagline
bolted on after the fact — it is the founding idea. Marco Taglione spent years in
Michelin-starred and 5-star luxury kitchens (Boscolo, Jumeirah, Antonello Colonna ⭐,
Cannavacciuolo ⭐) learning technique, and the restaurant exists to turn that
accumulated *sapere* into *sapore* on the plate.

Use this duality as the creative engine of the site:

- **Sapere** (knowledge) → technique, pedigree, study, precision, curiosity, the
  story of the chef and pastry chef.
- **Sapore** (flavor) → the dishes, the pairings, the pleasure, the experience at the table.

The best copy and design moments make *knowledge* and *flavor* meet in a single beat.

## Brand pillars

1. **Tecnica resa calore — Technique made warmth.**
   Stellar-level craft delivered in a place that feels welcoming, not intimidating.
   "Elegante ma non formale." Skill in service of pleasure and people.

2. **Creatività con radici — Creativity with roots.**
   Inventive, unexpected pairings (tuna kebab, seppia "tagliatelle", vanilla with
   scampi) that are bold but never gimmicky — each one is grounded in real cooking.

3. **Il mare, reinventato — The sea, reimagined.**
   A seafood-led kitchen with vegetarian options. Ingredient-driven, seasonal,
   respectful of the product, surprising in execution.

4. **Coppia, non catena — A couple, not a chain.**
   Founded and run by Marco and Marika. Personal, human-scale, intentional.
   Pastry is a first-class citizen, not an afterthought.

5. **Cura in ogni dettaglio — Care in every detail.**
   From HACCP/abbattimento notes to a 100+ label wine list to the dessert plating —
   excellence is in the details, and the website should reflect that same care.

## Positioning

- **Category:** Fine-dining-adjacent creative seafood restaurant in Frosinone.
- **For:** Couples, families, groups, and corporate dinners who want a memorable,
  out-of-the-ordinary meal without the rigidity of formal fine dining.
- **Unlike:** Both stiff white-tablecloth formality *and* generic trattorias —
  Sapore è Sapere offers starred-kitchen technique in a relaxed, characterful setting.
- **So that:** Guests feel they've had an experience worth talking about and returning to.

### One-sentence positioning statement

> For diners around Frosinone seeking something memorable, Sapore è Sapere is the
> creative seafood restaurant where Michelin-trained technique becomes warm, original,
> and accessible hospitality — because here, flavor is knowledge.

## Brand personality

If the restaurant were a person: a well-travelled, accomplished chef who is genuinely
warm at the table — speaks with quiet confidence, loves to explain a pairing without
lecturing, takes the food seriously but not themselves.

| We are…            | We are not…                    |
|--------------------|--------------------------------|
| Refined            | Stuffy / pretentious           |
| Warm, welcoming    | Cold, exclusive                |
| Confident          | Boastful, hype-driven          |
| Creative, surprising | Gimmicky, random             |

## Visual identity

The website uses an **elegant dark** direction, already established in `css/style.css`:

- **Palette:** charcoal/near-black background (`#14110f`), warm gold accents
  (`#c9a24b` / `#e3c987`), ivory text (`#ece6dd`), muted warm grey for secondary text.
  Gold = the spark of *sapore*; dark = the depth and seriousness of *sapere*.
- **Typography:** serif headings (Georgia/Times) for craft and tradition; clean
  sans-serif body for clarity and modernity. The pairing itself echoes *sapere + sapore*.
- **Logo mark:** a circular gold "S" monogram; full wordmark renders the *è* in gold to
  spotlight the wordplay.
- **Imagery:** dark, moody, close-up food photography; warm light; plenty of negative
  space. Let dishes and the chef be the heroes. (Assets live in `assets/images/`.)
- **Texture & space:** generous whitespace, thin gold dividers, subtle hover lifts.
  Restraint signals confidence — never crowd the page.
- **Motion:** minimal and tasteful (gentle hover transitions, smooth scroll).
  Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Speed is part of elegance.

## Signature verbal devices

- The wordplay **sapore / sapere** (use sparingly, never forced).
- Pairing language: "dove la tecnica incontra il calore", "il mare reinventato".
- Quiet credentials: name the starred kitchens as *story*, not as a trophy list.
- Italian first, always. Warm second-person plural ("vi aspettiamo") for hospitality.
